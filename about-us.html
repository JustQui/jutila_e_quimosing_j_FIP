About Us

Orbitz is a unique beverage brand that was first introduced in the 1990s. The brand quickly gained popularity for its unconventional and visually striking product design, featuring floating balls of flavour suspended in a colourful liquid. Despite its initial success, the brand was eventually discontinued due to low sales. However, in recent years, Orbitz has been given a new lease on life with a revamped product line that appeals to a modern audience.

The new and improved Orbitz is a product of careful research and development, with a commitment to quality and innovation at its core. One of the most notable changes to the brand is the addition of space debris to the drink. This is not just a gimmick, as the brand takes its inspiration from the cosmic world, aiming to create a drink that feels like a journey into outer space. The space debris is carefully selected and treated to ensure it is safe for consumption, adding a unique element to the product that is not found in any other drink.

Today, Orbitz is a brand that stands out for its one-of-a-kind taste and design. The new product line offers a variety of flavours that are sure to satisfy any palate, while still maintaining the distinctive floating ball design that made the brand so popular in the 90s. The brand has come a long way since its inception, but one thing that remains constant is its commitment to innovation and pushing the boundaries of what a drink can be. Orbitz is truly a drink brand that is out of this world!
